Our Story

We met each other a few times through our friends, John Brabant and Ashlee Willis, a couple years before we started dating. John was Jeff's college roommate at Northeastern, and both John and Ashlee went to high school with Jenna in Derry, NH. In July 2011, a group of friends went to the Cape for a weekend trip, and that's when we became friends. A few months later, Jeff asked Jenna to be his girlfriend, and Jenna replied with "Yes, maybe, some day." Eventually it became a full "yes"! Three years later, Jeff proposed in New York City under the Christmas tree at Rockefeller Center.
